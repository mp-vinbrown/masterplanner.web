Student Management

Grow Your School

Manage Your Student's Attendence

As your school grows and you sign up more and more students, keeping track of who is attending and who is not becomes a full-time occupation. It becomes very easy to lose track of students that haven't been in class for a long time. If students aren't in class they aren't likely to re-enroll. Master Planner lets you keep track of student attendence and will alert you to students that haven't been in class for a while.

Customized Ranking

As Your Students Make Progress, Your School Grows

Your School, Your Ranking System

Every school tracks progress differently. Aikedo, Tae Kwon Do, Karate, and all the others have very different belt ranking systems. Master Planner allows you to customize your school's unique ranking system and track your student's progress as they rise through the ranks.

Custom Email Campaigns

Communication is the Key To Your Success

Communication is the Key To Your Success!

Email can be the most difficult thing to manage, especially for large schools. You're in the business of teaching martial arts to your students, not email marketing. Yet time and time again we hear of the pain involved with managing email lists and sending scheduled emails for birthdays and anniversaries. Master Planner lets you manage these common email events or set up custom emails that you can repeat at regular intervals. You have as much or as little control as you want.
